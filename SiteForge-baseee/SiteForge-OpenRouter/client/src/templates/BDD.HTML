About Our Drive

LifeStream started in 2019 to ensure local medical centers always have a sustainable blood supply. Our campaign focuses on donor education, safety, and convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Healthy adults aged 18-65 who pass the eligibility quiz and have not recently donated are welcome!

Is it safe to donate?

Yes! All donations use sterile, disposable equipment handled by medical professionals.

How often can I donate?

Every 3 months, up to 4 times a year.
