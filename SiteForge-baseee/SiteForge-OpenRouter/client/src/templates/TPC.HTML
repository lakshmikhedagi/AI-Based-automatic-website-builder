About the Campaign

GreenRoots is a people-driven tree plantation campaign aiming to plant 100,000 trees by 2030, across cities, villages, and degraded lands.

  • Mission: Restore green cover through massive afforestation, community awareness, and sustainable care.
  • Vision: A healthy planet, thriving biodiversity, and resilient local communities.
  • Values: Participation, transparency, inclusivity, and stewardship of nature.
