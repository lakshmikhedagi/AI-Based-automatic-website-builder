About EarthWise

Our organization is dedicated to spreading climate awareness, inspiring individual and collective action, and supporting policies for a sustainable future.

Our Mission

  • Inform the public with science-based facts
  • Encourage eco-friendly habits and actions
  • Support local and global initiatives

Our Initiatives

  • Community Clean Energy: Supporting solar and wind projects locally.
  • Plant for the Planet: Organizing tree-planting events every quarter.
  • Eco Education: Conducting climate workshops and webinars.

Climate Resources & Action Tips
