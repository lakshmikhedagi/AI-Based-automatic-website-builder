Entrepreneur Stories

Read about the inspiring journeys of women changemakers across rural India.

Radha Kumari
After learning weaving from her grandmother, Radha began making eco-friendly baskets. Her village now exports crafts across the country.
Odisha
“My art keeps our tradition alive and supports my family’s dreams.”
Sangeeta Patel
Sangeeta harnessed organic farming practices to create high-quality pickles, jams and spices, delighting customers nationwide.
Gujarat
“Every jar is a taste of my land and hard work.”
Amina Bano
Amina designs vibrant block-printed fabrics, training local youth and expanding her business regionally.
Rajasthan
“My shop is a center for learning and creativity.”
