Clean Energy Facts

  • Renewable energy creates five times more jobs than fossil fuels.
    Source: Inspire Clean Energy
  • Wind turbines can provide electricity for up to 1,500 homes every year.
    Source: Inspire Clean Energy
  • Switching to 100% renewable energy could save billions in annual energy costs.
    Source: Inspire Clean Energy
  • Companies like Tesla, Google, and Walmart are leading green energy adoption.
  • Solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal are the most common renewable sources.

Adopting Clean Energy

  • Install solar panels or join a local community solar program.
  • Switch to a green energy provider if available in your area.
  • Enhance energy efficiency: use LED lights, smart thermostats, and energy-efficient appliances.
  • Promote public transportation, biking, or electric vehicles to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.
  • Spread awareness! Educate friends and neighbors about clean energy benefits.
